1. **Market Size**: Global AI agents market valued at ~$7.6-8.8B in 2025, expected $10.9B in 2026, projected $50-183B by 2030-2033 at 43-50% CAGR.
2. **Enterprise Adoption**: 57% of organizations have AI agents in production (up from 51%). 72% of Global 2000 companies operate beyond experimental. Gartner predicts 40% of enterprise apps embed AI agents by end of 2026.
3. **Developer Ecosystem Explosion**: Agent framework repos with 1000+ stars grew from 14 (2024) to 89 (2025) -- 535% increase. Framework usage surged 920% between 2023-2025.
4. **Open Source Dominance**: 68% of production AI agents built on open-source frameworks.
5. **Multi-Agent Interest**: 1,445% surge in multi-agent system inquiries Q1 2024 to Q2 2025 (Gartner).

1. **Single Agents -> Orchestrated Multi-Agent Systems**: Specialized agent teams replacing monolithic agents.
2. **Protocol Standardization (MCP and A2A)**: Anthropic's MCP and Google's A2A establishing foundational standards for tool/agent connectivity.
3. **Enterprise Pilot-to-Production Shift**: 2026 decisive transition. 80%+ Fortune 500 exploring agents.
